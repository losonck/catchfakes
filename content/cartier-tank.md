The Cartier Tank is a timeless symbol of elegance and sophistication, making it a prime target for counterfeiters. The WSTA0029 and WSTA0041 models, with their classic rectangular designs and refined aesthetics, are particularly susceptible to high-quality 'super-clone' replicas. These fakes can closely mimic the genuine articles in weight and feel, making it crucial to focus on micro-details and specific model characteristics to ensure authenticity. Given the high value of these watches, getting fooled by a counterfeit can be a costly mistake. This guide will help you identify the authentic features of these specific references, ensuring your investment is sound.

## Examine the Movement Caliber

The Cartier Tank WSTA0029 and WSTA0041 are powered by the Cartier calibre 1847 MC, an in-house automatic movement. When authenticating, ensure the movement matches this specification. Super-clones may use ETA-based movements that, while similar, lack the distinct finishing and layout of the 1847 MC. If possible, have a professional open the caseback to verify the movement's authenticity.

## Check the Caseback Design

Both the WSTA0029 and WSTA0041 feature a solid caseback. Authentic models will have precise engravings that include the Cartier name, reference number, and serial number. The engravings should be sharp and well-defined. Any signs of poor engraving quality or incorrect information can indicate a counterfeit.

## Inspect the Dial and Printing

The dials of these Cartier Tank models should exhibit flawless printing. The Roman numerals should be crisp and evenly spaced, with the 'VII' numeral subtly incorporating the Cartier name as a security feature. The minute track should be perfectly aligned with the indices. Any misalignment or smudging is a red flag.

## Evaluate the Crown and Cabochon

Cartier Tanks are known for their distinctive crowns set with a blue cabochon. The cabochon should be perfectly set and have a rich, deep blue colour. On fakes, the cabochon may appear dull or poorly set. Ensure the crown itself is well-crafted and operates smoothly.

## Analyse the Bracelet and Clasp

The WSTA0029 and WSTA0041 typically come with a high-quality leather strap with a deployant clasp. The clasp should be engraved with the Cartier logo and feel secure when closed. Check that the stitching on the leather strap is even and consistent. If the watch comes with a metal bracelet, verify that the clasp code matches the model's published code from manufacturer service literature.

## Scrutinise the Bezel Material

These references feature a stainless steel bezel. The finish should be flawless, with no signs of plating or wear that might suggest a lower-quality material. The edges should be sharp and well-defined, with a consistent polish or brushed finish depending on the model's specific design.

## Red Flags at a Glance

- Incorrect movement caliber → fake
- Poorly engraved caseback → fake
- Misaligned or smudged dial printing → fake
- Dull or poorly set cabochon → fake
- Inconsistent stitching on leather strap → fake

## Final Word

Authenticating a Cartier Tank WSTA0029 or WSTA0041 requires attention to detail and knowledge of specific model characteristics. While this guide provides key insights, it's always wise to seek a second opinion from an authorised dealer or independent watchmaker, especially when purchasing from non-authorised sources. A professional can provide an in-hand inspection that confirms authenticity beyond doubt.
