Six checks. Forty seconds. Catch nine of every ten Submariner fakes.

A reference-grade authentication walkthrough. Each check is independent, examinable in under a minute, and individually diagnostic. If three or more flag, the watch is almost certainly counterfeit.

CHECK 01 / 06

Dial Printing

Examine 'Submariner' and 'Swiss Made' under 10× magnification. Genuine printing is crisp, with no bleeding. Fakes often show feathering at the edges of small text.

DIAGNOSTIC: HIGH
CHECK 02 / 06

Cyclops Magnification

Genuine cyclops magnifies the date by 2.5×. Counterfeit lenses rarely exceed 1.8×. The date should also sit cleanly centered with no warp at the edges.

DIAGNOSTIC: HIGH
CHECK 03 / 06

Caseback

Solid stainless steel. No engravings. If you see Rolex logos or model text on the caseback, it is counterfeit — Rolex has not engraved casebacks since the early 90s.

DIAGNOSTIC: ABSOLUTE
CHECK 04 / 06

Movement

116610LN: calibre 3135. 126610LN: 3235. 124060: 3230. The Geneva striping, perlage, and red-gold rotor are visibly hand-finished — fakes are flat-stamped.

REQUIRES CASEBACK ACCESS
CHECK 05 / 06

Lume Application

Chromalight emits a clear blue glow. Counterfeit lume is greenish, fades within minutes, and is unevenly applied — particularly on the small dot pip on the bezel.

DIAGNOSTIC: MED
CHECK 06 / 06

Bracelet End-Links

The Oyster bracelet should fit seamlessly with the case — no gaps. The Glidelock clasp must engage with 24 distinct micro-adjustment clicks. Bracelet code engraved between lugs.

DIAGNOSTIC: HIGH

Field Notes

The Rolex Submariner is the most counterfeited watch in the world. Modern fakes — particularly the so-called "super clones" emerging from Guangzhou — have closed the visual gap to the point where photographs no longer reveal them. In-hand examination is now mandatory.

The good news: even the most accurate counterfeits still fail at least three of the six checks above. The dial printing, the cyclops magnification, and the bracelet tolerances are the most consistent tells. The movement, when accessible, is conclusive — but few buyers ever see it.
