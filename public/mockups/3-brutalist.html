CASE 001 // ROLEX SUBMARINER

Spot a fake Submariner in 6 checks.

The Submariner is the most counterfeited Rolex in circulation. Six checks — examined together — will catch nine out of every ten fakes. Even the convincing ones.

The Rolex Submariner is one of the most iconic luxury watches in the world. It is also the most counterfeited. With modern "super clones" closing the visual gap to genuine pieces, photographs alone no longer reveal a fake. In-hand inspection is now mandatory — and the six checks below are where you start.

1 — Dial Printing

Examine the dial under 10× magnification. Genuine Rolex printing is crisp, with no bleeding at the edges of small text. The 'Submariner' wordmark and 'Swiss Made' inscription should be perfectly aligned, with consistent kerning. Counterfeits often show feathering or off-center placement.

If three or more of these six checks FAIL, the watch is almost certainly counterfeit.

2 — Cyclops Magnification

The cyclops on a real Rolex magnifies the date by exactly 2.5×. Counterfeit lenses rarely exceed 1.8×. The date should sit cleanly centered with no warp at the edges of the lens.
